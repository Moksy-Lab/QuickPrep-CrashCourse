How it works

Foundation

Learn the basic

Start by building a strong foundation in digital marketing. You’ll explore essential topics such as SEO, advertising fundamentals, content strategy, and analytics. The focus is on clarity, giving you a structured overview to begin your journey with confidence.

Engagement

Engage with Lessons

This week, dive into focused video modules led by industry experts. Each lesson is designed to be completed in under 30 minutes, making it ideal for busy schedules. Track your progress and begin connecting theory with practical insight.

Application

Implement Using Toolkits

Apply your new skills to real-world scenarios using ready-made templates and downloadable toolkits. Whether it’s building a campaign or analysing performance data, you’ll put learning into practice for immediate impact.

Progression

Evaluate and Advance

Complete short assessments to validate your understanding and uncover areas for growth. Receive expert tips, prepare for certifications, and refine your skills to confidently advance your digital marketing goals.

Key topics in the crash course

Introduction to Digital Marketing

Learn the basics of digital marketing and how it compares to traditional methods. Explore digital channels like social media, email, and SEO, and discover how to integrate them into your business strategy to improve reach, engagement, and conversions in today’s competitive online landscape.

Explore More

Social Media Marketing

Understand how to use Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and LinkedIn to build your brand and connect with audiences. Learn how to create engaging content, run organic and paid campaigns, measure performance, and use each platform’s unique features to grow awareness and drive targeted results.

Explore More

Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

Master the core strategies of SEO to boost website visibility. Learn keyword research, on-page optimisation, link building, and technical SEO. Improve your site’s ranking on Google and attract high-intent traffic that converts into leads or customers through consistent, search-friendly practices.

Explore More

Content Marketing and Strategy

Discover how to create compelling content that informs, entertains, or converts. Learn blogging, video creation, and campaign planning. Understand how to build a content calendar, choose the right format for your audience, and distribute content effectively across digital platforms to maximise reach.

Explore More

Email Marketing and Automation

Explore the fundamentals of email marketing for building relationships and driving sales. Learn to grow your list, segment your audience, craft personalised emails, and automate campaigns. Track performance metrics and continuously optimise your approach to improve open rates, engagement, and ROI.

Explore More

Paid Advertising (PPC)

Learn how to set up and manage paid campaigns on platforms like Google Ads and Meta. Understand keyword targeting, bidding strategies, ad copywriting, and performance tracking. Use PPC to drive quick, measurable traffic and conversions while staying within budget and optimising spend.
