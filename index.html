How it works

Foundations

Learn the basic

In the first week, participants will dive into the core principles of digital marketing. Topics include understanding key channels such as SEO, social media, and email marketing. Learners will also focus on defining target audiences, creating buyer personas, and setting measurable goals to build a solid foundation for the rest of the course.

Strategy

Plan the path

Week 2 is dedicated to building a strategic marketing plan. Participants will learn how to select the right platforms, create effective content strategies, and position their brand in the market. The week also covers competitor analysis, ensuring that learners can develop a comprehensive and data-driven marketing strategy.

Execution

Execute campaigns

In Week 3, participants will start executing their strategies by launching campaigns across various channels. This includes hands-on experience with social media marketing, SEO, paid ads, and email campaigns. Practical tools like Google Ads and Mailchimp will be introduced to help learners implement and manage their campaigns effectively.

Optimization

Join real world projects

The final week focuses on optimizing marketing campaigns. Learners will analyze campaign performance using analytics tools and KPIs, then refine their strategies with techniques like A/B testing and audience segmentation. Participants will also work on practical projects to apply their knowledge, ensuring they can adapt their strategies for real-world success.
